About the Team
Beacon is built by quantitative trading professionals who understand that the gap between simulation and production is where strategies fail. After years of building institutional trading infrastructure at tier-1 firms, we created Beacon to solve the fundamental challenges in strategy development.

Why Beacon Exists
Traditional backtesting fails because it can't simulate what actually breaks strategies: competitive dynamics, realistic execution timing, and infrastructure stress. Beacon provides competitive live backtesting where multiple strategies compete for the same liquidity in real-time.

What Makes It Different
• Near tier-1 infrastructure speeds — 3.1μs P99 platform latency
• Multi-strategy competition — Test against multiple algorithms simultaneously
• Dynamic execution — Match's Expeditor configures liquidity consumption realistically
• No static trades — Everything is generated dynamically with proper message ordering
• Real protocols — CME, NASDAQ, NYSE exchange-accurate implementations
• Disaster simulation — Stress-test with network failures, gaps, and edge conditions

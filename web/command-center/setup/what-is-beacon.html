What is Beacon?

Professional HFT simulation platform built for speed, precision, and ultimate flexibility.

Intelligent Defaults. Complete Control.

Beacon starts by allowing you to create custom datasets for your Live HFT Simulation. You choose the products. Beacon provides intelligent defaults using OHLCV data. Customize product-specific parameters, such as price and quantity ranges and bid-ask spread percentages. At the click of a button, Beacon will generate a trading day's worth of data. All in the exchange protocol of your choice. Reuse existing datasets, or create new ones to satisfy your backtesting needs.

Professional HFT Simulation Platform

Beacon is a web-based platform that provides live HFT simulation with production-grade performance. Unlike traditional backtesting, Beacon simulates the complete trading infrastructure in real-time.

How It Works

1. Configure Symbols and Market Scenarios

Select your instruments and trading scenarios - Choose from popular symbols with intelligent defaults based on actual OHLCV data, or add custom symbols with your own price ranges, volatility, and volume patterns.

2. Select Exchange Protocol

Choose CME, NASDAQ, or NYSE - Beacon generates your market data in the native binary format for your selected exchange.

3. Deploy Your Dataset

Start the simulation - Beacon begins streaming your generated market data over UDP to localhost, ready for your algorithm to consume.

4. Connect Your Algorithm

Plug-and-play integration - Your algorithm receives the UDP stream of your generated dataset and sends TCP orders exactly as it would in production. No infrastructure setup required.

Why This Matters for HFT

Traditional simulation requires months of infrastructure work and manual symbol configuration. Beacon provides production-grade testing (4.5μs tick-to-trade) with intelligent defaults for popular instruments - no calibration work required.

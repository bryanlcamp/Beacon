Beacon's Pipeline. Adding Your Quotes.

  • →Beacon's Pipeline starts at Beacon's Playback application.
  • →No configuration required on your part.
  • →Most importantly.
  • →Per your config, Playback adds quotes to the Pipeline.
  • →Both your strategy and Beacons's Expediter will see quotes.
  • →Likely one will react and message Beacon's Matching Engine.
  • →All messages sent to the Matching Engine are sent to Beacon's Pipeline.
  • →Note that both execution messages and quotes maintain a strict order in the Pipeilne.
  • →Specifically, new quotes aren't sent from the Pipeline prior to execution messages.
  • →The same is true for all messaege types, including trades, that impact the book.

Competition Option 1: Automatch.

  • →If your strategy has no competition, then execution is futile.
  • →Beacon't Matching Engine offers a configurable Automatch.
  • →The Matching Engine listens to your quote stream.
  • →Automatch processes quotes faster as it runs inside the Matching engine.
  • →It can be configured to be as aggressive as you'd ilke.
  • →Note that you can run a handful of Automatchers per product.

Competition Option 2: Multiple Strategies.

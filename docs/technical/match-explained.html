Match.

Match is your strategy's order execution exchange.

Match, like your strategy, listens in as Pulse is broadcasting your dataset containing bid/ask prices. It posts these prices as liquidity in its order book.

Hence, Match will post liquidity at the same time your strategy sees the new price. However, to actually execute on this new price, remember that when your strategy sends an order it must make an extra TCP/IP hop to get there. And by that time the liquidity could be gone.

Match provides you with built-in competition called Expediter. You can configure this feature such that it crosses the market to get filled, be first in line then cancel, the market moves, and you might stuck with an unwanted fill. etc.

Check viability. Build. Iterate. Scale.

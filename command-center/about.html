The Vision

Beacon represents the culmination of years of experience in high-frequency trading technology. Built from the ground up to solve the real challenges of algorithmic trading development, testing, and deployment in today's ultra-competitive markets.

With sub-microsecond latency capabilities and enterprise-grade reliability, Beacon provides the foundation for serious algorithmic trading strategies that can compete at the highest levels.

Technical Excellence

Every component of Beacon is engineered for performance. From lock-free message passing to zero-allocation hot paths, the system achieves consistent 3.5μs end-to-end latency while maintaining deterministic behavior under extreme market conditions.

Core Technologies
  • Modern C++20 with concepts and consteval
  • DPDK for kernel bypass networking
  • Custom memory allocators and object pools
  • Real-time market data processing
  • High-precision timing and latency measurement
Development Philosophy

Beacon is built on the principle that trading technology should be both powerful and accessible. Complex underlying systems are wrapped in intuitive APIs that let strategy developers focus on alpha generation rather than infrastructure concerns.

The platform encourages rapid experimentation while maintaining production-ready reliability. Whether you're backtesting new ideas or deploying live strategies, Beacon provides the tools and confidence you need to succeed.

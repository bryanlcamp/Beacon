Strategy Interaction

Your strategy interacts with the Matching engine by submit, update and cancel order operations.
You will receive an immediate ack or reject and your strategy can act accordingly.
The Matching Engine notifies you immediately when you're filled, an unsolicited cancel or trading halt.

Automatch

Sometimes you want your strategy to compete for liquidity. Not always take it all.
The Automatcher addresses this by matching orders based on their time spent in the queue.
Your strategy is notified of these matches via trade messages in the quote stream.

Scalability

The Matching Engine has multiple extension points; so you can adapt it as your needs change.
Run competing strategies simultaneously. Desired market-making behavior, or order types.
Built with flexible architecture, enhancements feel natural, not forced.

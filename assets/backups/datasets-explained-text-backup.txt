Datasets Explained.
Datasets are the HFT Marketplace. All Quotes. Curated by You.

Beacon's Market Data Generator is the exclusive application for creating datasets.
Datasets contain quotes from a single exchange. NASDAQ, NYSE, or CME.

Dataset Properties.
- Synthetic quotes based on your criteria.
- Binary files, adhering to exchange specs.
- Streamed using Beacon's Playback.
- Intended to be saved and reused.

Datasets are not.
- Aware of, or contain any trades.
- Knowledgeable of how they're being sent.
- Modified after being created.
- Anything more than raw data.

Creation. Usage. Flow.
Create: Dataset Generator, Set Options, Generate.
Stream: Dataset Playback, Open Dataset, Playback Options, Play (UDP Loopback).
Consume: Algorithm Container, Decode Quote, Algorithm Code...

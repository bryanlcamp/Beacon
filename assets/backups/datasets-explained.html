Datasets, Explained.

Purpose. Contents. Creation. Usage. The marketplace.

Dataset Properties.

  • →Binary files with quotes for multiple products.
  • →Created by you using the Dataset Generator.
  • →Configure bid/ask price/qty ranges/weights.
  • →Create trends and mean reversion.
  • →Configurable by product or by dataset.

Exchange Protocol.

  • →Datasets are encoded per exchange specs.
  • →Datasets support one exchange per file..
  • →CME, NASDADQ, and NYSE supported.
  • →Exchange protocol version is config-driven.
  • →Beacon translates emulating exchanges.

Save and Reuse.

  • →Auto-saved for future replay.
  • →Auto-purged to save disk space.
  • →Save config files for future tweaks.
  • →Takes seconds to create.
  • →Built with quick iteration in mind.

Usage in Beacon.

  • →Choose a dataset in Dataset Playback.
  • →Configure playback options and start.
  • →Quotes go to Strategy & Matching Engine.
  • →Matching Engine has instant liquidity.
  • →Strategy receives and reacts to quotes.

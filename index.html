Beacon generates realistic market scenarios from the patterns you specify. One quiet Tuesday becomes fifty unique quiet Tuesdays. Your strategy learns market structure, not memorized sequences.

The conditions that break strategies aren't in textbooks—they're in the millisecond when three algos hit the same level. Beacon puts you there until you know how to react.
